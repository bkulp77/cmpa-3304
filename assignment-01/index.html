Top 5 List

  1. Major League

    A hilarious comedy about a Major League baseball team that is built to lose so the owner can move the team

  2. Field of Dreams

    A movie about a man that has dreams about building a baseball field in his cornfields and baseball players from the past come back to play on it

  3. For the Love of the Game

    A movie about a baseball player who is struggling with his career and personal life and on the verge of pitching a perfect game at the end of his career.

  4. The Natural

    A movie about a young baseball player who is discovered by a veteran player and becomes a star

  5. The Sandlot

    A movie about a group of kids who play in a local sandlot and learn important life lessons
